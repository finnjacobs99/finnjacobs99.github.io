Athletic History

Throughout elemntary and middle school I took part in most regular team sports that people play, like basketball, soccor, baseball, etc. I never really stuck with them due to the fact that I wasn't anywhere close to athletic. Some would even refer to me as a benchwarmer. Since team sports never worked out for me I would find most enjoyment in skateboarding, rock climbing, and gymnastics, until one day when I was I gave skiing a try. I instantly fell in love and wanted to become a professional like the guys you see in the X-Games, and to this day I still chace after that goal. I also was a member of my highschool diving team junior and senior year, but I didn't enjoy diving as much as I just liked hanging out with the team during practice. After diving my senior year I've decided to quit, since it was during ski season and I wasn't able dive half the season due to injuries from skiing.
